# Governance

Kazane is currently governed by ZYX Corp during early concept, prototype, and dogfooding phases.

## Maintainer responsibility

Maintainers are responsible for preserving the design intent of Kazane, reviewing responsibility-boundary changes, maintaining ADRs for significant decisions, and preventing the project from shrinking into a generic task manager or chatbot wrapper.

## Decision process

Major changes should be documented through ADRs.

ADR-required changes include runtime architecture, persistence model, AI agent permission model, UI strategy, licensing, deployment assumptions, context and evidence data models, RDE or audit model changes, and public positioning changes.

## Human responsibility

AI agents may assist with design, implementation, review, and documentation, but final governance decisions are made by human maintainers.
