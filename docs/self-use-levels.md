# Self-use Levels

Kazane should be used to build and operate Kazane, but not only for development. Self-use means ZYX uses Kazane across its own workflows, including product design, documentation, development, operations, AI番頭 preparation, and external feedback.

## Principle

**Kazane is built through Kazane.**  
**Kazane is not governed by Kazane alone.**

AI may assist, but humans remain responsible for governance and high-risk decisions.

## Levels

| Level | Version | Scope | Meaning |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 0 | v0.0 | Concept | Kazane is not yet used directly |
| 1 | v0.1 | Record | Internal Work Items, including Kazane work, are recorded |
| 2 | v0.2 | AI flow | AI picks up assigned business Work Items and returns Handoffs |
| 3 | v0.3 | Provenance | Decisions, Evidence, and Context updates are preserved |
| 4 | v0.4 | Development proof | Kazane manages GitHub/CLI/MCP-oriented development flows |
| 5 | v0.5 | Internal cross-functional | ZYX uses Kazane across development, docs, strategy, operations, and customer-like work |
| 6 | v0.6 | Audit | RDE and Evidence Audit are applied to Kazane work |
| 7 | v0.7 | AI番頭 | AI番頭 integration work is managed through Kazane |
| 8 | v0.8 | External feedback | Partner and beta feedback becomes Context and Work Items |
| 9 | v0.9 | Productization | Pricing, trust, onboarding, support, and docs are managed in Kazane |
| 10 | v1.0 | Closed loop | Kazane development, operation, feedback, and improvement circulate through Kazane |

## Safety rules

- AI cannot finalize changes to Kazane's design philosophy.
- AI cannot finalize public claims.
- AI cannot finalize license or legal policy.
- AI cannot change responsibility boundaries without human approval.
- Done requires handoff, evidence, and context update where applicable.
